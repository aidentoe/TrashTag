Our Story

How a simple observation turned into a mission to clean our planet

The Inspiration

As high school students, we were constantly frustrated by seeing our local parks and walkways filled with litter, especially after storms when garbage would accumulate unchecked.

We realized that traditional cleanup efforts weren't engaging enough to inspire consistent participation, and that's when we came up with the idea to combine environmentalism with technology and gamification.

"We wanted to make cleaning up fun and rewarding - turning an environmental responsibility into an engaging challenge that people would want to participate in."

Our Tech-Driven Solution

TrashTag was born from our vision to create a platform that makes environmental cleanup:

  • Gamified - Earn points, complete challenges, and climb leaderboards
  • Collaborative - Work with friends, organizations, and your community
  • Visible Impact - Track your environmental contributions in real time

We built TrashTag using modern web technologies to ensure it's accessible to everyone who wants to make a difference.
